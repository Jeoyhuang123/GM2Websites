Marketing

üîò Target Audience

Our focus from the beginning has been on the local community, which extends from the Winchester campus to the entire town, and even engages audiences interested in nature and environmental protection issues.

What we want to convey to the audience is the impact of nature, the environment, and waste items (recyclable and non-recyclable items) generated by human activities on it, but we do not focus on negation or criticism, nor do we express a positive attitude. We unilaterally and objectively Starting from the perspective, it reflects its significance and may trigger the audience's thinking. In a certain respect, it is a media display with educational practical significance.

Since we want to present our ideas in a more realistic way, we use mobile phone protective films, which are extremely common in daily life, as mobile phone simulators.

Firstly, we can shorten the distance between the audience and the work; secondly, the materials we use are from daily life. Things that can be found everywhere, such as chargers, straws, shopping receipts, plastic bottles, glass products, etc., are combined with materials in the natural environment to achieve the theme concept of "skeuomorph" or "coexistence".
